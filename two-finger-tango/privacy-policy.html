Privacy Policy

App: Two-Finger Tango  |  Developer: Vnworks  |  Effective Date: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Two-Finger Tango ("the App") handles your information. Please read it carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

Two-Finger Tango does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account registration, login, or network connectivity to play.

All gameplay, scoring, and leaderboard data are stored locally on your device and never leave it unless you explicitly choose to share a score image (see Section 4).

2. Local Storage

The App stores the following data on your device using the operating system's standard local preferences storage. This information stays on your device only and is not transmitted anywhere:

  • Your personal best score (high score).
  • A local leaderboard of up to five entries per difficulty, each containing the score and a short nickname (up to 8 characters) that you voluntarily enter when you beat your previous best.
  • The most recently selected difficulty level, used to pre-select the same option next time you open the App.
  • The last nickname you submitted, used to pre-fill the name entry field on subsequent record screens.

You can erase all of this data at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

3. Permissions

The App requests only the minimal permissions required for its core functionality:

  • Vibration — used to provide haptic feedback during gameplay (near-miss, combo, game over).

No other sensitive permissions (internet, location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage access, advertising ID, etc.) are requested by the App. The App does not require an internet connection to function.

4. Score Sharing

When you break your personal best, the App offers an optional "Share" button that generates a score image on your device and hands it off to your operating system's standard share sheet. You then choose whether, and to which app (for example, Messenger, Facebook, or photo gallery), to send it.

This action is entirely user-initiated. The App itself does not upload the image, and it does not communicate with any third-party service on your behalf. Once the image is handed to the destination app you choose, that app's own privacy policy applies.

5. Advertising and Analytics

Two-Finger Tango does not contain advertising, analytics, crash reporting, tracking SDKs, or any other third-party data collection libraries. No advertising ID is read or used.

6. Data Sharing

Vnworks does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Because the App does not collect personal data in the first place, there is no data for us to share.

7. Data Retention

Since the App stores score and preference data only on your own device, Vnworks has no access to it and retains nothing on its servers. Removing the data is fully within your control via your device's app-storage settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
