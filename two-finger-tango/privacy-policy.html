Privacy Policy

App: Two-Finger Tango  |  Developer: Vnworks  |  Effective Date: April 19, 2026  |  Contains ads (Google AdMob)

This Privacy Policy explains how Two-Finger Tango ("the App") handles your information. Please read it carefully before using the App.

Summary: Vnworks does not collect any personal data directly. Gameplay and leaderboard data stay on your device. The App shows advertisements served by Google AdMob (a banner on the home screen and an occasional full-screen interstitial between plays); AdMob may collect advertising-related data such as your advertising ID and IP address, as described in Section 6.

1. Information We Collect

Vnworks itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account registration or login.

All gameplay, scoring, and leaderboard data are stored locally on your device. The App connects to the internet only to fetch advertisements through Google AdMob (see Section 6); any advertising-related data is collected and processed by Google under its own privacy terms.

2. Local Storage

The App stores the following data on your device using the operating system's standard local preferences storage. This information stays on your device only and is not transmitted anywhere:

  • Your personal best score (high score).
  • A local leaderboard of up to five entries per difficulty, each containing the score and a short nickname (up to 8 characters) that you voluntarily enter when you beat your previous best.
  • The most recently selected difficulty level, used to pre-select the same option next time you open the App.
  • The last nickname you submitted, used to pre-fill the name entry field on subsequent record screens.

You can erase all of this data at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

3. Permissions

The App requests only the minimal permissions required for its core functionality:

  • Vibration — used to provide haptic feedback during gameplay.
  • Internet — used solely to load advertisements served by Google AdMob (see Section 6).

No other sensitive permissions (location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage access, etc.) are requested by the App.

4. Score Sharing

When you break your personal best, the App offers an optional "Share" button that generates a score image on your device and hands it off to your operating system's standard share sheet. You then choose whether, and to which app (for example, Messenger, Facebook, or photo gallery), to send it.

This action is entirely user-initiated. The App itself does not upload the image, and it does not communicate with any third-party service on your behalf. Once the image is handed to the destination app you choose, that app's own privacy policy applies.

5. Analytics

Two-Finger Tango does not contain analytics, crash reporting, or any tracking SDKs other than the Google AdMob advertising SDK described in Section 6.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob (a banner on the home screen and an occasional full-screen interstitial between plays). To serve personalised or non-personalised ads, Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID / iOS IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage and interaction data related to ads

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Vnworks. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google's advertising policies.

7. Data Sharing

Vnworks does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. The only third-party data flow in the App is the Google AdMob SDK described in Section 6, which operates under Google's own privacy terms. Because Vnworks does not collect personal data in the first place, there is no data on our side to share.

8. Data Retention

Score and preference data is stored only on your own device, so Vnworks has no access to it and retains nothing on our servers. Removing this data is fully within your control via your device's app-storage settings. Any advertising-related data collected by Google AdMob is subject to Google's own retention policies.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
