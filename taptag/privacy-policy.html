Privacy Policy

App: TapTag  |  Developer: Vnworks  |  Effective Date: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TapTag ("the App") handles your information. Please read it carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

TapTag does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account registration or login of any kind.

All NFC operations (scanning and cloning tag UIDs) are performed locally on your device. No NFC tag data is sent to any server.

2. NFC Usage

The App uses the device's NFC hardware solely to:

  • Read the UID and basic information of nearby NFC tags.
  • Clone a tag UID onto a compatible writable NFC card.

This data is processed entirely on-device and is never uploaded or shared with any party.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

TapTag displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve personalized or non-personalized ads, Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage and interaction data related to ads

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Vnworks. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google's Advertising policies.

4. Permissions

The App requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • NFC — required to read and write NFC tags.
  • Internet — required solely for loading AdMob advertisements.

No other sensitive permissions (location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, etc.) are requested.

5. Data Sharing

Vnworks does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. The only third-party service integrated in the App is Google AdMob, which operates under its own privacy terms as described in Section 3.

6. Data Retention

Since TapTag does not collect any personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete on our end. Any ad-related data collected by Google AdMob is subject to Google's own retention policies.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
